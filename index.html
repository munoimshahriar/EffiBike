About Us

Effi Bike is a dynamic Canadian brand , specializing in family-friendly and leisure e-bikes, as well as models perfectly suited for delivery workers. In addition to selling our own brand, we provide repair and rental services and cater to both individual and business customers, offering delivery solutions tailored to families and professionals alike. Effi Bike is dedicated to providing top-quality service and innovative e-bikes for a wide range of needs. Whether you're commuting, delivering, or enjoying a weekend ride, Effi Bike has the perfect solution for you.
